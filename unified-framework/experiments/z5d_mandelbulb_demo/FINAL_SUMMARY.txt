TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

Core Innovation:
  Z5D prime-counting curvature κ(n) ≈ d(n)·ln(n)/e²
  Extended to R³ via: κ(p) ≈ log(|px|)·log(|py|)·log(|pz|)·log(|p|)/e²
  
  Result: Spatial complexity prediction from number theory

Adaptive Stepping:
  High κ(p) → complex geometry → small steps (conservative)
  Low κ(p)  → smooth space   → geodesic leaps (10-1000× faster)
