# Overview

## What is the Z Framework?

• **Unified mathematical model** that bridges physical and discrete domains through the empirical invariance of universal constants (speed of light, golden ratio)

• **Prime prediction system** using geodesic transformations θ′(n,k) = φ·{n/φ}^k that achieves orders of magnitude better accuracy than classical Prime Number Theorem estimators

• **Cross-domain analysis framework** applying geometric resolution to replace fixed ratios with curvature-based geodesics for enhanced pattern detection

• **High-precision computational platform** using mpmath (50-digit precision) for numerical stability in ultra-extreme scale analysis (validated up to k = 10^10)

• **Scientific research tool** with reproducible benchmarks, bootstrap confidence intervals, and clear separation between empirically validated results and theoretical hypotheses
