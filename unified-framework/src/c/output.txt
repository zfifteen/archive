What This Benchmark Does:
========================
This tool measures the performance impact of parallel (OpenMP) and vectorized (SIMD)
optimizations on the Z5D predictor algorithm. It processes large batches of prime
predictions (100M per test) to accurately measure computational performance benefits.

The benchmark:
• Runs Z5D predictions sequentially (single-threaded)
• Runs the same predictions in parallel (multi-threaded with OpenMP)
• Measures wall-clock execution time for both approaches
• Calculates speedup ratios and performance improvements
• Generates CSV telemetry data for further analysis

Why This Matters:
================
Prime prediction algorithms like Z5D are computationally intensive. Understanding
how well they scale with modern multi-core processors is crucial for:

• Performance Optimization: Quantify benefits of parallelization strategies
• Hardware Selection: Determine optimal CPU configurations for prime research
• Algorithm Validation: Ensure optimizations don't compromise mathematical accuracy
• Scalability Planning: Predict performance on larger datasets and systems
• Research Efficiency: Reduce computation time for mathematical discoveries

The results help researchers and developers make informed decisions about
computational approaches for large-scale prime number analysis.
