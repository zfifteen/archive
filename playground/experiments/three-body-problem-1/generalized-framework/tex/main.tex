\section{Introduction}
The $Z_Q$ diagnostic family provides a computationally efficient bridge between numerical simulation and Hamiltonian perturbation theory. By measuring the normalized flux through level sets of a confinement functional $Q$, we obtain a direct probe of Nekhoroshev stability.

\section{Theory}
\subsection{Theorem 1: Stability}
In the Nekhoroshev regime, flux is exponentially small. (See \texttt{theorem1\_nekh.tex})

\subsection{Theorem 2: Diffusion}
In the resonance-overlap regime, flux is proportional to the diffusion coefficient. (See \texttt{theorem2\_diffusion.tex})
