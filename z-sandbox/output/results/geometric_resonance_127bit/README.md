## Method Verification

### Prohibited Operations - NOT USED

The following classical factoring operations are **explicitly excluded** from the search loop:

- ❌ **ECM** (Elliptic Curve Method)
- ❌ **NFS** (Number Field Sieve)
- ❌ **Pollard** (p-1, rho, or other Pollard methods)
- ❌ **GCD cycles** during candidate generation
- ❌ **Library factoring** (sympy.ntheory, gmpy2, factordb, etc.)

### Permitted Operations - USED

The following geometric and mathematical operations **are** used:

- ✅ **Comb formula** for candidate prediction: p_m = exp((ln N - 2πm/k) / 2)
- ✅ **Dirichlet kernel** for resonance detection: D_J(θ) = Σ_{j=-J}^{J} exp(ijθ)
- ✅ **Golden-ratio QMC** for k-parameter sampling
- ✅ **Integer rounding** at final candidate snap
- ✅ **Divisibility testing** (N % p) at final stage only

### Instrumentation

The `method.py` script includes verification checks:

1. **Import verification:** Checks that no prohibited factoring libraries are loaded
2. **Loop verification:** Comments marking each operation to show it's pure geometric
3. **Modulo restriction:** The only modulo (%) operation is in final divisibility check
4. **No GCD:** No greatest common divisor computations in search loop
